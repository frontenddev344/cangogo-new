Things to do

Canada boasts a wealth of incredible attractions, some of which are among the finest on the planet. Be sure to explore our comprehensive guide to the top activities and experiences available in Canada.

Explore Banff National Park

Located in the Canadian Rockies, Banff National Park offers stunning mountain landscapes, hiking trails, and opportunities for wildlife viewing.

Visit Niagara Falls

One of the most iconic natural wonders in the world, Niagara Falls is a must-see destination with boat tours, viewing platforms, and nearby attractions.

Discover Old Quebec

Explore the charming and historic streets of Old Quebec City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its European architecture and culture.

Experience the CN Tower

In Toronto, you can visit the CN Tower, an iconic skyscraper with an observation deck offering panoramic views of the city and Lake Ontario.

Tour Vancouver

Vancouver boasts beautiful parks, a vibrant arts scene, and outdoor activities. Stanley Park and Granville Island are popular attractions.

Go Whale Watching

Canada's coasts are known for incredible whale-watching opportunities. Places like Victoria, British Columbia, and Tadoussac, Quebec, are known for their whale-watching tours.

Explore the Maritimes

Visit the Maritime provinces of Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and Prince Edward Island for stunning coastal scenery, historic sites, and fresh seafood.

Experience the Northern Lights

Head to the Yukon, Northwest Territories, or parts of northern Quebec to witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights (Aurora Borealis).

Discover Montreal

Enjoy the rich culture, cuisine, and festivals in Montreal, Quebec's largest city. Don't miss the historic Old Montreal area.

Visit Ottawa

Canada's capital city is home to landmarks like Parliament Hill, the National Gallery of Canada, and the Canadian Museum of History.

Hike in the Rockies

Beyond Banff, explore other areas of the Canadian Rockies, such as Jasper and Yoho National Parks, for more breathtaking landscapes.

Go Skiing

Canada offers world-class skiing and snowboarding opportunities in places like Whistler, British Columbia, and Mont Tremblant, Quebec.

Experience Indigenous Culture

Learn about Canada's Indigenous peoples' rich history and culture through museums, cultural centers, and events.

Cruise the Inside Passage

Take a cruise through British Columbia's Inside Passage to witness stunning coastal scenery, fjords, and wildlife.

Enjoy Local Cuisine

Sample Canadian specialties like poutine, butter tarts, Nanaimo bars, and local craft beers in various regions across the country.
